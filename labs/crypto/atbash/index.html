Atbash Cipher

Atbash is a simple substitution cipher: each character maps to its mirrored position in the alphabet (first ↔ last, second ↔ second-to-last, ...). It's symmetric: encryption and decryption are the same operation.

Note: Atbash is its own inverse, so Encrypt/Decrypt produce the same result — the toggle is here for consistency across the lab.
